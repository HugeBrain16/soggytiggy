Commissions

Commission process:

  1. If I'm open for commissions, dm me (on instagram, twitter, or email me: soggytiggy@gmail.com) and tell me your idea (may not respond immediately).
  2. I will decide if I will do it or not, and I can offer changes if I can't draw something.
  3. payment upfront, I will work on it once I see the money in my bank, after which you will receive a sketch. In this stage you can ask for big changes.
  4. I will finish the commission in a span of few days, you can ask for small changes.
  5. After the commission is finished, I'll send you the finished piece with a big watermark and after the other half of the payment is done, I'll send you the one with small, non-intrusive watermark.
  6. Depending on the commission, I may or may not post it on my account. (You get to choose whether I @ you or not).
